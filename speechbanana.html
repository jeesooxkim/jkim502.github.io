Department of Biomedical Engineering

Speech Banana is an exercise-based application designed to aid deaf individuals who have received cochlear implants. These individuals have lingering impairment in communication due to underdeveloped listening skills. Through easily accessible systemized listening exercises, we aim to improve their communication abilties, and consequently, their quality of life.

I am the developer of the Android version of the app, and I am also in charge of maintaining the iOS version. In the future we plan to implement gaming components to the chapter exercises to create an engaging user interface, in order to encourage younger users. We are also seeking to increase our reach within the deaf community by working on a British English version and a Korean version of the application. We are currently undergoing preparations for clincal trials.
